# RISK

## Security Risks
- Risk: bypassing bridge-governed execution path.
- Control: every execution unit must keep intent/policy/evidence/trace linkage.

## Privacy Risks
- Risk: simulation payload may include unintended personal data in future extensions.
- Control: Phase 1 stores governance-safe summary fields only.

## Operational Risks
- Risk: deterministic mismatch between replays.
- Control: verdict hash regression tests and stop-ship on mismatch.

## Legal Risks
- Risk: monetization boundary drift without high-tier review.
- Control: Phase 1 explicitly excludes payment and premium currency; any boundary expansion escalates to high.

## Approval Tier Mapping
- Change class: 2D runtime UX/economy balancing within soft-currency scope.
- Required tier: medium.
- Rationale: bounded gameplay policy updates with owner review.

- Change class: price/odds/monetization boundary update.
- Required tier: high.
- Rationale: economic and compliance impact requires mandatory architect gate.
