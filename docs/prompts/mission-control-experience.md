# Mission Control Experience

Treat the landing as an operator console, not a marketing splash.

For `v0.8.0`:

- make the system feel live, stateful, and reviewable
- prefer panels, rails, status lamps, and command surfaces over generic showcase blocks
- let motion reinforce hierarchy and signal flow, not distract from it
- keep the page legible on mobile and stable under reduced motion

The atmosphere should feel experimental, but the information architecture must stay disciplined.
